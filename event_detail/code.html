About this Event

Join us for an interactive Sign Language Workshop hosted by the Disability and Academic Support Services (DASS). This session is open to all Ashesi students, staff, and faculty who want to learn the basics of Ghanaian Sign Language (GSL) and American Sign Language (ASL).

The workshop will cover essential greetings, academic vocabulary, and tips for inclusive communication on campus. No prior experience is required.
