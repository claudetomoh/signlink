Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request an interpreter for a class?

expand_more

From your dashboard, tap "Request Interpreter". Choose the event type, select the date and time, then confirm. Your request will be reviewed by the DASS coordinator who will assign an available interpreter.

How far in advance should I submit a request?

expand_more

We recommend submitting requests at least 48 hours in advance. For urgent requests within 24 hours, contact the DASS office directly at dass@ashesi.edu.gh.

Can I chat with my interpreter before the session?

expand_more

Yes! Once an interpreter is assigned, you can message them directly through the app using the Messages tab or the Chat button in your schedule. You can also start a video call from there.

What if I need to cancel or change a request?

expand_more

You can cancel or edit a pending request from your Schedule page. Once a request is approved, please notify the DASS office as early as possible to allow reassignment of the interpreter.

How do I upload my semester timetable?

expand_more

Go to your dashboard and tap "Upload Timetable". You can upload a PDF, image, or Excel file. Download your official timetable from the Ashesi student portal for best results.

How do I reset my password?

expand_more

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your Ashesi email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
